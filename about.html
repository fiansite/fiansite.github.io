Al Fian

He has been a voracious reader for books especially science fiction or fantasy since 15. The classics like the Hobbits, the Lord of the Rings, and the Dunes series along with the Alchemy and later on Harry Potter series opened up his imagination. Such stories have accompanied him from a young boy to an adult when he’s ready to tell his own stories. For now, storytelling isn’t his profession yet but a serious hobby. He’s been an IT manager for over 15 years. He got a master’s degree in computer science and a bachelor’s degree in physics.
